BOM:

· 14x Thonkiconn Jacks + nuts
· 12x 2mm LEDs
· 1x 100K
· 4x 47K
· 16x 10K
· 12x 2K2
· 12x 1K
· 1x 100nF
· 1x 10uF electrolytic
· 2x 1N4148W
· 16x MMBT3904
· 1x CD4024 TSSOP package
· 1x CD4017 TSSOP package
· 5x2 pin power header (SMD)

- All resistors and capacitors SMD size 0603.

- The LED corresponding to the /2 output is oriented
the same direction as the ones below.

- It may be necessary to shorten the pins of the power header
by slightly cutting them to make it match the pads on the board.
Or you can use a regular straight pin header and just bend the
pins 90º as I did.
